All About Chess!



A Brief History of Chess

Chess is the world's most popular and recognized games, and originated around 1500 years ago in India. It quickly become popular, and spread throughout the ancient world. As time went on, the rules changed and the game evolved. The modern chess that we all play today originates from Europe, and is slightly different from the original chess in India. This website will teach you how to play the modern chess we all know of today, as well as hints, tips, and tricks to make you a good player.



How Do You Play Chess?

So you may be wondering. Even if I know or just learned all the rules of Chess, how exactly do I access the game in order to play it? Well, Chess is usually played physically with a board and physical pieces, however you don't exactly need a physical set of chess to play chess. We have the internet today, which of course, contains online chess. Many websites host chess avaliable for you to play online against other real people or bots, allowing you to practice and hone your skills. You can play competitively against other players from around the world, or play casually with your friends.

Online Chess

There are plenty of websites that host Online chess, however the most popular is chess.com ,which is the top rated chess site, and you can see why. It offers plenty of bots for you to train against, game analysis, competitive multiplayer chess with a HUGE playerbase, more chess minigames, and even forums and chats!

Chess.com is a great online chess website, however it isn't the only one. While others may be slightly the same level, a bit better, or a bit worse, Chess is Chess, and there will be many great alternatives, with our top 3 alternatives being:

  1. lichess.org
  2. FIDE chess
  3. Duolingo chess: Great for learning!

But in reality, any chess app on your phone or simple website works, because the game is played the same way, with the same rules. You can even try online chess in this website! We've included an html chess game here.
